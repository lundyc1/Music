Biography

Chris Lundy is currently studying Interactive Multimedia at The College of New Jersey. Both as a solo artist and with accompaniment from Lisa Palacio, Chris has written and recorded original and cover material. His debut album “Collected Recordings” was released independently on January 23 of 2014. Featuring vocalist Lisa Palacio, the album included two originals which will be reintroduced on the up and coming EP in the summer of 2014.

Originally from Pequannock, New Jersey, Chris has been playing drums for around 10 years and guitar for 6. Major inspirations for his playing technique and style include his father, Chuck Lundy, Joe Satriani, Brian Setzer, John Pizzerelli, and Paul Gilbert.

All recoding is done on a home-studio budget. This includes 2 Blue Yeti multipurpose microphones, a MacBook Pro with various music production software, and a 12 input digital preamp/mixer. Instrument wise, recording is done mostly with a T-BUCKET™ 400CE Flame Top electric acoustic from Fender and a JS100 series (transparent red) electric from Ibanez.
